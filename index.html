History

Kit Kat was first created in the United Kingdom in 1935 by Rowntree's of York, a confectionery company that is now owned by Nestl√©. The original flavor was milk chocolate, and the name "Kit Kat" is thought to have been inspired by a popular 18th-century literary club called the Kit-Cat Club.

Over the years, Kit Kat has become a global phenomenon, with different flavors and variations available in countries all over the world. Some popular flavors include hazelnut, strawberry, and caramel.
